Paneer Tikka Masala

Smoky grilled paneer cubes in a vibrant, tangy masala — the star of every vegetarian menu.

⏱ 40 min 🍽 Serves 3 📊 Easy 🌍 Punjabi
← Back to All Recipes

📋 Recipe Info

Prep Time20 min
Cook Time20 min
Total Time40 min
Servings3 people
DifficultyEasy
CategoryVegetarian
Calories~290 kcal

🛒 Ingredients

  • 400g paneer, cubed
  • 1 cup thick yogurt
  • 2 tsp tikka masala powder
  • 1 tsp turmeric
  • 1 tsp red chilli powder
  • 1 tbsp ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 tbsp mustard oil
  • 1 large capsicum, cubed
  • 1 large onion, cubed
  • 1 tsp chaat masala
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh mint chutney, to serve

👨‍🍳 Step-by-Step Instructions

This recipe works beautifully on a grill, under a broiler, or in a well-heated non-stick pan.

  1. 1

    Prepare the marinade: In a large bowl, combine thick yogurt, tikka masala powder, turmeric, red chilli powder, ginger-garlic paste, mustard oil, lemon juice, and salt. Whisk until smooth.

  2. 2

    Marinate paneer & vegetables: Add paneer cubes, diced capsicum, and onion pieces to the marinade. Gently mix to coat everything evenly. Cover and rest for at least 20 minutes.

  3. 3

    Thread the skewers: Alternate paneer, capsicum, and onion pieces on skewers or toothpicks. This keeps them together during cooking.

  4. 4

    Grill or pan-cook: Heat a grill pan or tawa on high. Lightly brush with oil. Place skewers on the pan and cook for 3–4 minutes per side until nicely charred and golden.

  5. 5

    Finish and season: Remove from heat, dust with chaat masala, and squeeze a little extra lemon juice over the top.

  6. 6

    Serve: Plate immediately with mint chutney, sliced onion rings, and lemon wedges. Best enjoyed fresh off the grill!

💡 Pro Tip

Use hung curd (strained yogurt) for the marinade — it sticks better and gives a thicker, more flavourful coating on the paneer.
